Armed with certifications in Google Data Analytics and AWS Cloud Practitioner, I am well-equipped to tackle challenges in data science and analytics roles, leveraging my technical acumen and analytical prowess to drive informed decision-making and business growth.

Through my diverse range of projects, I've cultivated a robust skill set that spans Data Analysis, Machine Learning, Database Development, and Application Development. This combination of skills not only enables me to formulate action-oriented observations from complex datasets but also to build reliable and effective predictive, prescriptive and simulation models that drive informed decision-making and contribute to the success of analyses and projects.
